Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Ready Tote Oklahoma wants everyone to be able to use our website and our service, including people who use assistive technologies. We aim to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA on this site.

What We've Done

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper headings, landmarks, and labels
  • A "skip to main content" link for keyboard and screen reader users
  • Descriptive alternative text on all meaningful images
  • Form fields with visible labels and keyboard-accessible controls
  • Visible focus indicators for keyboard navigation
  • Color contrast chosen for readability against our backgrounds
  • Reduced-motion support: the site respects your device's "reduce motion" setting
  • Text that scales with your browser's zoom and font-size settings

Known Limitations

Our delivery area maps are interactive and may be difficult to use with a screen reader. All of the information shown on the maps is also available as text on the same page: the list of cities and zip codes we serve. If you're unsure whether we deliver to your address, call or email us and we'll tell you directly.

Booking Without the Website

Every part of our service is available by phone. If any part of this site is hard for you to use, you can reserve totes, ask questions, and manage your rental entirely by calling 580.399.3202 or emailing readytoteok@gmail.com.
